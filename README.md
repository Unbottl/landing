# Privacy Policy for Unbottl

**Last Updated: March 10, 2026**

Unbottl ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Unbottl mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Information We Collect
To provide our "Emotional Mirror" and "Clarity Report" services, we collect the following:
* **Audio/Transcript Data:** When you "vent," we process your voice-to-text data to provide AI-driven feedback.
* **User Inputs:** Information you provide during onboarding (issues, intensity, commitment).
* **Usage Data:** Diagnostic data and app interaction patterns via Firebase.
* **Authentication:** Basic identifier data managed through Firebase Authentication.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
* **AI Processing:** We use Google Gemini (via Google AI Studio) to analyze your vents and provide empathetic validation and actionable insights.
* **Personalization:** To tailor the "Mirror" experience to your specific emotional knots.
* **App Improvement:** Using anonymized diagnostics to fix bugs and improve the audio engine.

## 3. Data Storage and Security
* **Encryption:** All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using Firebase Industry-standard security.
* **Privacy Shield:** We do not sell your personal emotional data to third parties. Your "vents" are yours.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party providers:
* **Google Firebase:** For backend hosting, authentication, and analytics.
* **Google AI Studio (Gemini):** For processing transcripts to generate "Clarity Reports."
* **Apple App Store:** For subscription management and payments.

## 5. Your Rights
You may request the deletion of your account and associated data at any time through the App settings.

## 6. Children's Privacy
Unbottl is not intended for use by children under the age of 13.
